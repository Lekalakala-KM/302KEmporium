Quality CMT, clothing manufacturer and retailer

302K Emporium is a leading clothing manufacturer specializing in the bulk production and supply of uniforms, corporate wear, personal protective equipment (PPE), sportswear, linen, and headwear. We pride ourselves on our commitment to delivering high-quality products and exceptional service while building a customer-centric business that caters to a global audience.

Our CMT Studio is located in the heart of Polokwane CBD, and while we primarily operate within the greater Limpopo region in partnership with local distributors, our reach extends to customers around the world. We utilize reputable courier services to ensure timely delivery and satisfaction for our international clientele.

As a high-volume manufacturer serving all genders and age groups, we maintain substantial stock levels to facilitate rapid distribution with minimal waiting periods. Our continuous market and manufacturing research enhance our product knowledge, allowing us to provide valuable offerings that resonate with customers globally and ensure that our clothing label is well-received in diverse markets.

At 302K Emporium, we understand that our strength lies in our people. We invest in building strong, engaged teams dedicated to producing and delivering reasonably priced products and services that reflect the quality and exclusivity our brand represents. We utilize high-end materials such as cotton, wool blends, and high-gauge denims, guaranteeing premium products that customers worldwide are happy to invest in, thanks to their exceptional quality and return on investment.
